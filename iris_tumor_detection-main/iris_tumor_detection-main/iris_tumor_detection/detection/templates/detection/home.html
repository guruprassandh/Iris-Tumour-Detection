What is an Iris Tumor?

An iris tumor is an abnormal growth in the iris, which is the colored part of the eye. These tumors can be benign (non-cancerous) or malignant (cancerous). Early detection and treatment are crucial for better outcomes.

Awareness about iris tumors is essential for timely intervention. Regular eye check-ups and understanding the symptoms can help in early diagnosis.
